The Zero (0) Complexity Business IT Design principles

零 (0) 复杂性业务 IT 设计原则

This 0CX (zero complexity) standard defines architecture and design principles to prevent complexity for new products.

这个 0CX（零复杂性）标准定义了架构和设计原则，以防止新产品的复杂性。

Put People first!

以人为本！

Only use what you understand.

只使用你理解的东西。

Define specific criteria that are tangible to measure complexity.

定义衡量复杂性的具体标准。

Create a model of your solution

创建解决方案模型

Separation of concerns

关注点分离。

Reduce all waste.

减少所有浪费。

Problems should be fixed through simple solutions.

问题应该通过简单的解决方案来解决。

Design for change.

为改变而设计

Make sure you can manage IT!

确保您可以管理 IT！

Privacy by design.

隐私设计

Never over engineer

永远不要过度设计
